doves

this page is dedicated to doves

doves are a biblical symbol of the Holy Spirit. a notable example is when a dove brought an olive branch to Noah after the flood, indicating that land was near. this represents God's peace and forgiveness.

in another part of scripture, the Holy Spirit is described as "descending as a dove". the imagery of the dove, with its white wings and round features, evokes feelings of purity, otherworldliness and gentleness.

did you know that pigeons are multi-colored doves? they are common in New York because people used to keep them as pets back in the day.
